My toolkit

List of tools I use nowadays for most of the project related activities.

UserZoom

I've used services of that company for quite a few projects. I know how to create task based tests, surveys and tree tests.

Adobe Analytics

I use it for most of my projects. I know how to create basic reports.

Google Analytics

I mainly use it for my personal projects and for small tests. I understand it on basic level.

HotJar

I am using this tool since I was one of the beta testers back in 2014. I love how easy it integrates with Axure wireframes. Which allows me to track and measure them when I test my projects internally with co-workers. William Hill is a large organization so I collect a lot of valuable insights.

Axure RP 8

This is my favourite tool. I use it for diagrams, lo and hi fidelity wireframing and prototyping. I've been using it for a couple of years now and I feel confident enough to call myself an expert.

Sketch

I started using Sketch a year ago, when I began working for William Hill. I know it well enough to create wireframes, use most of its features and communicate design choices with the rest of the team.

Invision app

Sketch & Invision go hand in hand in terms of hi-fidelity prototyping. This toll is fairly easy to use.

Slack

This is my favourite tool for communications between team memebers.

Trello

Me and my team use this tool to manage our department's tasks on daily basis. I even use it for my personal projects and family tasks.

Jira & Confluence

In each company I ever worked for Jira remains one of the most important tools for task and issue management. The same goes for Confluence. We use to communicate business requirements, annotate designs and manage project documentation.
