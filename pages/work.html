Registration & Log in process

2016/2017 @ William Hill

Registration is one of the first steps in User onboarding process. This is a type of project which never ends. There is always something we could optimize or A/B test. I am a member of a team consisting of; Data Analyst, UI Designer and PO, continuously working on optimization of the entire registration journey.

I managed to increase registration conversion by 18% by hiding the promo code field. Instead of User testing we decided to use A/B testing to verify our assumptions and build a better product.

Currently I am working on a hypothesis that splitting the process into three steps will further increase conversion.

Casino Management System

2012 @ Novomatic

This was one of the most unique and interesting projects in my career. It didn't involve a typical design for desktop or mobile. Instead I was working to deliver a solution for On-the-floor gaming machines.

Casino managers have to solve a variety of problems while working in a busy environment such as the Casino floor. One of the biggest challenges they were facing was handling overflowing coins in a machine.

Together with the team of researchers I spent quite a lot of time on the floor, taking notes, observing and shadowing managers while they were working. The initial idea to help solve their problem was to utilise the tablets they were carrying around at all times. After some exploration we decided to discard it in favour of a system accessible from any machine on the floor with the turn of a key.
